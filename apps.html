Lightfire

Lightfire is the ultimate e-book reader. It has a support for a number of ebook formats such as epub, pdf, fb2, mobi, prc, txt, rtf, and much more. Lightfire syncs your books, highlights, progress, so you can pick up where you left.

Radarwave

Radarwave provides you suggestions based on your location. Radarwave learns your culinary taste, clothing style, and all places you love, to provide you with a suggestions tailored to you and where you are.

Sunbeam

Sunbeam is both a powerful and simple app to check the weather at your exact location. Sunbeam presents the weather to you with the most beautiful visualizations. It can even notify to bring an umbrella before leaving your home!
